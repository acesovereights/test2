Requirement 1

The color scheme for this website is based around a black(#000000) / red(#ED1C24) / and parchment paper(#C18D23) scheme with the text being graphite gray (#454343) for a softer, less harsh feel to the text. The overwhelming color will be parchment paper brown in the background image. The logo is black and red with a bit of white.

The top header will be graphite gray with a red shadow, while the footer will be black with a red shadow to add contrast in the text and make it legible on the background.The navigation link icons on the left are green hop cones. I thought about making them red to fit the color scheme and tie into the logo but they ended up looking too much like small strawberries.

The overall theme of the site is a vintage feel. Water stained notebook covers, worn paper, and "hand written" recipes. Despite the nostalgic feel, I wanted the logo to be a modern splash of color in the Trask Polka style.
